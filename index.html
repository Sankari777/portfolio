About Me

Hi, I'm Sankari, a passionate web designer and developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. My journey began as a Computer Science and Engineering student, where I discovered my love for creating interactive and visually appealing websites.

Over the years, I’ve honed my skills through various projects and achievements:

  • Science Day Project: Led a team to develop a voice-controlled robot, which won second prize for innovation and functionality.
  • Naan Mudhalvan Initiative: Built a Salesforce app titled “Supply Leftover Food to Poor”, addressing food waste and hunger.

My goal is to create meaningful web experiences that solve real-world problems and inspire users. I’m continuously learning and excited to bring ideas to life through code and design.
